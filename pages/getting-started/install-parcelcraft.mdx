# Installing Parcelcraft from the Stripe Apps Marketplace

> Parcelcraft is currently in private beta and is not available for installation in the Stripe App Marketplace yet. To get instructions for becoming a Beta user, email [support@parcelcraft.com](mailto:support@parcelcraft.com). 

Parcelcraft is conveniently available in the Stripe Apps Marketplace, making installation a breeze. Follow these simple steps to add Parcelcraft to your Stripe account:

## Step 1: Open the Stripe Apps Marketplace

There are two ways to access the Stripe Apps Marketplace:

1. In your Stripe dashboard, click on the "+" icon located on the right side of the screen, then click the "Explore Apps" button, as demonstrated in the animation below.

![Open Stripe Apps](https://parcelcraft.com/images/open-stripe-apps.gif)

2. Alternatively, you can directly navigate to the Stripe Apps Marketplace by clicking on [**this link**](https://marketplace.stripe.com/).

## Step 2: Search for Parcelcraft

Once you're in the Stripe Apps Marketplace, locate the search bar and type in "Parcelcraft." Press Enter to initiate the search.

## Step 3: Install Parcelcraft

From the search results, find the Parcelcraft app listing and click on the "Install app" button. Stripe will guide you through the installation process, which may include granting necessary permissions for Parcelcraft to integrate with your Stripe account.

## Step 4: Configure Settings

After successfully installing Parcelcraft, you will be automatically redirected to the app's settings page. This is where you'll configure various options to customize Parcelcraft according to your shipping needs. Don't worry; the next pages in this tutorial will walk you through the settings in detail.

### Accessing Parcelcraft on Mobile Devices

Please keep in mind that Stripe Marketplace Apps, including Parcelcraft, are not currently supported within the Stripe mobile app. However, you can still use Parcelcraft on your phone or tablet by accessing the Stripe web page through a mobile browser.

By following these installation steps, you'll be ready to start using Parcelcraft and revolutionize your shipping process directly from your Stripe account.
