## Accessing the Parcelcraft Settings

Navigate to the Parcelcraft Settings page by following these steps:

1. Click on the Parcelcraft icon ![Parcelcraft Icon](https://parcelcraft.com/images/favicon-32x32.png) in your Stripe dashboard.
2. In the Parcelcraft app, click on the "..." menu in the top-right corner.
3. Select "View app settings" from the dropdown menu.
