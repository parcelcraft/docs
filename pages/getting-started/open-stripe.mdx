# Setting Up Your Stripe Account

Parcelcraft is a powerful shipping tool designed as a Stripe Marketplace App, which means it seamlessly integrates with your Stripe account. To use Parcelcraft, you'll need to access it through your Stripe account's web interface.

## Stripe Account Requirements

If you don't have a Stripe account yet, don't worry! Setting one up is a simple process:

1. Visit [**Stripe.com**](https://dashboard.stripe.com/register) to create your account.
2. Follow the prompts to provide your business information and complete the registration process.
3. Once your account is set up, you'll be ready to install and start using Parcelcraft.
